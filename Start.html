PROFESSIONAL SERVICE

ClearPath Outdoor was founded by a Purdue Landscape Architect with 25 years experience in outdoor projects.   We hope to offer exceptional service at an affordable price.  We can also help with any question you might have about your landscape.


OWNER OPERATED

We're an owner operated business. You'll work directly with the owner throughout your project.  Have a question?  Please ask!

RESPONSIVE

We will reply to your request today and most projects are scheduled to start the same week.  With powerful equipment, we can work quickly to minimize our time at your home.

PRO EQUIPMENT

We're equipped with the latest stump grinding equipment. Our compact machine will allow us to reach stumps with little to no lawn damage. We can also easily fit through a 36" gate.

SAFE AND INSURED

We follow pro safety standards, including utility location, personal safety gear and screen guards around our machine to protect your home. We're also fully insured to work on your property.

RECYCLE!

We'll grind your stump and surface roots to a depth of 8"-12", leaving you with a neat pile of wood chips in it's place. Your new mulch can be used later in a natural bed area or compost pile. Recycle your tree stump!
